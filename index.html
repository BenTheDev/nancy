About NancyPeters

Nancy Peters is a dynamic and results-driven filmmaker and producer who has grown through the ranks since starting in 2018, progressing from production manager to associate producer and now producer. With experience across short films, commercials, and music videos, she brings a well-rounded production perspective and is known for transforming creative concepts into compelling visual stories. Committed to collaboration, efficiency, and excellence from pre-production through post, Nancy remains passionate about storytelling and continuously seeks opportunities to grow, learn, and deliver outstanding results on every project.

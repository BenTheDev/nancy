About Nancy Peters

NancyPeters is a dynamic and results-driven filmmaker, known for her ability to turn creative concepts into compelling visual stories. With experience spanning across various roles in production from associate Producer to Producer she brings a well-rounded perspective to every project. Nancy is deeply committed to maintaining a collaborative, efficient, and positive work environment, ensuring that each department functions in harmony from pre-production through post.

Nancypeters has worked on a numerous projects, started film maker from the year 2018, growing through the ranks from production manager to associate Producer, and now Producer. She has gained valuable experience producing on short films, commercial and music video alongside various production companies.

Nancypeters is passionate about storytelling, dedicated to delivering excellence on every project, and is constantly striving to raise the standards of her craft. She's looking forward to giving her very best on every new opportunity, while also remaining open to learning, growing, and collaborating with other creative in the industry
